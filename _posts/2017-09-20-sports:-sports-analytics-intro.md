# Welcome to the Club

Our goal: celebrate your love for sports with us, learning and applying technical 
skills, and start answering the difficult questions.

Just trying to win fantasy football? Trying to go to attend [MIT Sloan Sports Analytics
Conference](http://www.sloansportsconference.com/) with original research? Get
involved with DSA.

## How to Get Involved

Look for our blitz on Campus Listserv! Meetings are typically Thursday at 8:00pm.

## Conference Opportunities

Dartmouth College supports the club in both on-campus and off-campus projects.
This includes potential financial aid to attend conferences around the country:

- [MIT Sloan Sports Analytics Conference](http://www.sloansportsconference.com/) (February)
- [Carnegie Mellon Sports Analytics
  Conference](http://www.cmusportsanalytics.com/conference/) (October)
